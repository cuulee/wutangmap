About this Map

On a cold Wisconsin evening, Katie Kowalsky pondered a deep question:
What does the geographical representation of the Wu Tang Clan look like?

As a cartographer (and 90's rap enthusiast), she decided to create a custom geographical dataset and analyze the geography of rap. She created this dataset by using SQL/PostGIS to create a couple of GeoJSONs and used leaflet to make a web map showing the locations. After the map was created, she decided to use SQL/PostGIS to do some data analysis on the geographical distribution of the points she had gathered and used D3 to represent some of her findings.

So that's how this map got made. If you feel like I missed something on the map, feel free to contribute or contact me on twitter.
